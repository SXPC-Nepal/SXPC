We Create Opportunities
and Events

As a student council, we have been organizing various events and programs that have brought together national and international scientists, professors, and students to share their knowledge and experiences.

As a member of the IAPS, we have access to tons of opportunities, hence helping out students and members to inspire, guide, and grab those opportunities.
